Trytet Engine Benchmarks

These charts display raw measurement data from local development benchmarks. They reflect preliminary, workload-specific observations — not peer-reviewed results. Precision figures (confidence intervals, fitted curves) are omitted because the sample size is too small for meaningful statistical inference.
